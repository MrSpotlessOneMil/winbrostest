# Cleaner Portal — Phase 2

Follow-up to the Telegram removal + cleaner portal launch (March 2026). Everything below is planned but not built yet.

---

## Pay Tracking Dashboard
- Earnings per job, per week, per month
- Tips tracking (from Stripe tip flow)
- Bonuses (attendance, upsell, referral)
- Pending vs paid status
- Payout history

## Reviews Showcase
- Aggregated from Google reviews + customer feedback
- Per-cleaner rating visible on their portal
- Highlight top reviews

## Gamification
- Leaderboard (monthly, all-time)
- Streaks (consecutive on-time arrivals, 5-star ratings)
- Badges (first job, 50 jobs, perfect month, etc.)
- Upsell bonuses (cleaner suggests deep clean upgrade, gets cut)
- Attendance bonuses (no cancellations in X weeks)
- Completion milestones

## Customer Notes Per Home
- Persist across recurring jobs (gate code, pet info, parking, "don't move the rug")
- Cleaners can add notes from the portal
- Notes show on job detail page for next cleaner

## Before/After Photos
- Cleaners upload photos from job detail page
- Attached to the job record
- Viewable on dashboard by owner
- Optionally sent to customer in post-job follow-up

## Route Map View
- Google Maps directions from current location to job address
- Multi-job day: optimized route view
- ETA display

## Cleaner Referral Program
- Cleaners refer other cleaners
- Tracking via unique referral code
- Bonus payout after referred cleaner completes X jobs

## Portal UX Polish
- Push notifications (service worker / PWA)
- Offline checklist support (sync when back online)
- Dark mode
- Calendar view of upcoming jobs
- Availability editor improvements (drag-to-select time blocks)
